Hello!

Hello! I am a Graduate student at USC's Viterbi School of Engineering(joined Fall 15) pursuing a Master's Degree in Computer Science.

My interests include distributed systems, mobile applications and databases. Recently, I have also worked on systems based project - FaSteR Spaceman. This project aimed at improving the freespace mapping utility and the capabilities of the online defragmentor of the linux filesystem - XFS. During the course of this project, I also got a chance to work closely with Dave Chinner who is the current maintenance head at XFS and also a developer at Red-Hat.

My other application projects are linked to the academic scene, first being a complete testing environment(adaptive) for the Akshara International School, India and other being a fully developed Virtual Learning Environment where professors can upload study material and assignments.

Personally, I am big Tennis and Roger Federer fan. I am also passionate about the automobile world.

To delve deeper in my areas of interests, I have taken various courses from MOOCs like coursera and edX. Cloud Computing Concepts by Indy Gupta and Social Network Analysis by Lada Adamic being my favorites. Recently I have also completed a web design edX course- Learn HTML5 from W3C which covered topics such as HTML5, CSS and JavaScript.
